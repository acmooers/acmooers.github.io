About Amber

Amber Mooers is a multidisciplinary sculptor originally from Massachusetts. Her work is both playful and uncomfortable, drawing inspiration from her relationship to her body and her tumultuous upbringing. She is inspired by the surreal nature of dreams, as well as practical effects in classic horror movies.

While ceramics is her primary medium, she enjoys experimenting with fibers and glass in tandem with clay, and finds great satisfaction in the science behind her processes. She currently works and teaches in Los Angeles California.
